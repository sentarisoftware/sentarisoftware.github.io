# Case Study: Electrical Contractor Website + Lead Pipeline Integration

## Client Profile
**Organization:** Central West End Electric — St. Louis electrical services company  
**Challenge:** Lost leads from website form submissions, no visibility into customer inquiries, manual tracking  
**Solution:** Professional website with automated lead capture, CRM integration, and automated reporting

## The Problem

Central West End Electric's online presence was costing them customers:

- **Lead loss:** Website contact form submissions disappeared into email inbox black holes
- **No follow-up tracking:** Staff couldn't see which leads were contacted or left hanging
- **Manual reporting:** Monthly lead activity required spreadsheet updates
- **Unprofessional appearance:** Outdated website didn't build customer trust
- **No competitive advantage:** Competitors' websites looked more professional

**"We knew we were losing qualified leads — they'd fill out the form and we'd never know until they called someone else."** — CWE Operations Manager

## The Sentari Solution

Sentari didn't just build a website — we built a **lead capture and operations system**:

### What Was Built:
- **Professional website** — Modern, mobile-responsive, SEO-optimized design
- **Automated lead capture** — Every form submission instantly logged to Lead Pipeline
- **Watchtower monitoring** — 24/7 uptime, SSL expiry, and form health checks
- **Self-healing infrastructure** — Automatic redeployment if services fail
- **Monthly automated reports** — Lead activity summary sent to client automatically
- **Update intake system** — Customers can request updates through a hosted form
- **Never-lose-a-lead resilience** — Silent-until-needed Gmail alerts for failures
