What This Solves

The problem every document-heavy office knows.

The Before Picture

A shared inbox with 50-200 documents arriving weekly. Staff use sticky notes to track what's theirs. No one knows what's stuck, what's waiting, or who's handling it. Managers spend hours answering "where are we on this?" Documents get lost between people and steps.

Your staff retypes the same data from PDFs into your system. Every. Single. Time. Errors creep in. Deadlines slip. Compliance risk rises. And you can't hire fast enough to keep up.

The After Picture

Every incoming document is read automatically. Key fields are extracted into a clean, searchable queue. Staff see their assigned work with clear status. Managers see the whole workflow at a glance. Nothing gets stuck because someone forgot.

Your staff review and approve — they don't retype. Errors drop because machines handle the data entry. Everything is tracked and auditable. And you get your hours back.
